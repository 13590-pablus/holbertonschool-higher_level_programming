================================
How to use 4-text_indentation
================================

>>> text_indentation = __import__('4-text_indentation').text_indentation

>>> text_indentation("Hello. How are you? I am fine: good.")
Hello.
<BLANKLINE>
How are you?
<BLANKLINE>
I am fine:
<BLANKLINE>
good.
<BLANKLINE>

>>> text_indentation("   Space before. And after.   ")
Space before.
<BLANKLINE>
And after.
<BLANKLINE>

>>> text_indentation("No punctuation here")
No punctuation here
